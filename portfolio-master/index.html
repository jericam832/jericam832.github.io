About Me

My name is Jerica Mitchell and I am an up and coming web developer based in the Houston, Texas area (Go Texans!!! 🏈). I have a passion for problem solving and designing. I design responsive websites and web applications.

I have a bachelor's degree in Information Technology. With my background in IT, I am able use gui and command line interfaces and work with Git, Angularjs, Nodejs, MongoDB, RESTful, and many other front-end and back-end technologies.


I am constantly learning new things I can use within my projects. My goal is to learn as much as I can and produce awesome applications geared toward helping people. I am currently freelancing and I am open to a full-time position at with a great organization. I have so many great ideas for applications that I want to share with the world.
